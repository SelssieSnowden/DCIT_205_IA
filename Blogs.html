BLOGS

Greetings from the Dynamic World of Computing!

Welcome to the official blog of the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana. Here, we share insights, updates, and exciting news about the vibrant activities within our department. Whether you're a current student, alumni, prospective student, or simply an enthusiast of the ever-evolving world of computer science, this blog is your window into our community.

Program Highlights:


Explore the unique features of our MSc., MPhil., and Ph.D. programs. Discover the accelerated master's experience and the immersive research journey of our Ph.D. candidates. Learn about the specialized courses, capstone projects, and the dynamic curriculum that sets our programs apart.

Research Spotlights:


Delve into the cutting-edge research taking place within our department. Our faculty and students are actively contributing to Intelligent Space and Machine Learning, Data Science/Big Data Analytics, Networking and Communications Engineering, and Cybersecurity. Get a glimpse of the innovative projects shaping the future of technology.

Student Experiences:


Follow the journeys of our students as they navigate the challenges and triumphs of their academic pursuits. Hear about internships, industry collaborations, and the diverse paths our graduates take in the world of computer science.

Tech Talks and Seminars:


Stay informed about our regular seminars where students and faculty delve into thought-provoking discussions, review ongoing research, and critique advancements in the field. Engage with the latest trends and breakthroughs in computer science.

Connect with Us:


Don't miss out on the opportunity to connect with us on social media. Follow our Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn pages to stay updated on the go. Join the conversation using our official hashtag: #UGComputerScience. We invite you to explore, engage, and be inspired by the dynamic world of computer science at the University of Ghana. Thank you for being a part of our community!
