Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

>Welcome to the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana! Below, we've compiled a list of frequently asked questions to help you navigate through the information you may need. If you have additional questions or require further clarification, feel free to reach out to our department office.

1. What programs does the Computer Science Department offer?

We offer a Master of Science (MSc.) in Computer Science, Master of Philosophy (MPhil.) in Computer Science, and a Doctoral (Ph.D.) program in Computer Science.

2. What is the duration of the MSc. and MPhil. programs?

The MSc. program is a 1-year weekend/evening program, while the MPhil. program is a 2-year full-time program. The first year of the MPhil. is focused on taught courses, and the second year is dedicated to research and dissertation.

3. Can I pursue the MSc. or MPhil. program part-time?

Yes, both the MSc. and MPhil. programs are designed to accommodate both full-time and part-time students.

4. What is the duration of the Ph.D. program?

The Ph.D. program is a full-time research program with an expected duration of 4 years.

5. Are there opportunities for financial assistance or scholarships?

Information on scholarships and financial assistance can be obtained through the University's scholarship office. Additionally, some research and teaching assistant positions may be available within the department.

6. How can I connect with faculty members for research opportunities?

We encourage you to explore faculty profiles on our website to identify potential research mentors. Feel free to reach out to them directly or attend departmental seminars and events to connect with faculty members.

7. What research areas does the department focus on?

Our research areas include Intelligent Space and Machine Learning, Data Science/Big Data Analytics, Networking and Communications Engineering, and Cyber Security.

8. Are there opportunities for student involvement in research projects?

Yes, students are encouraged to join research labs and participate in ongoing research activities within the department.

9. How can I stay updated on departmental events and activities?

Visit our department website regularly for updates on events, seminars, and other activities. You can also follow us on social media for real-time announcements.
